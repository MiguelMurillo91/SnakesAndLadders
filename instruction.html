Game Instructions

Objective

  • Be the first player to reach the final square by moving your token according to the dice rolls.

    • Game Rules

  • Players take turns to roll a standard six-sided die and move their token forward the number of spaces rolled.
  • If a player lands on the bottom of a ladder, they climb to the top of the ladder.
  • If a player lands on the head of a snake, they slide down to the tail of the snake.
  • The first player to reach or exceed the last square is the winner.

    • Setup

  • Each player chooses a token and places it on the starting square.
  • Decide who goes first, and then take turns clockwise.

    • Winning

  • The first player to reach or exceed the final square is declared the winner!
