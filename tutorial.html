Snakes and Ladders Tutorial

Rule 1: Each player starts at the bottom of the board.

Rule 2: Players take turns rolling a dice and moving forward the number of spaces shown on the dice.

Rule 3: If a player lands on a ladder, they climb up to the higher-numbered space.

Rule 4: If a player lands on a snake, they slide down to the lower-numbered space.

Rule 5: The first player to reach the top of the board wins.
